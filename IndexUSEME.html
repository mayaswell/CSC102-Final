Grow more of your own food and lower your grocery bill!

Money does not create oxygen! No matter how much cash resides in your pockets, bank account, or trust fund, it won't keep your pantry stocked if there isn't anything on the shelves in a grocery store. Growing a small amount of your own food can lower your monthly spending, provide additional nutrition you might be lacking, and allows for a sense of security in uncertain times. A lot of plants only require a small amount of space and can be grown anywhere, even indoors! Something as simple as growing a basil plant in your windowsill is the first step in sustaining yourself and your household. Here are some basic staples that can be grown by beginners and green thumbs alike.
